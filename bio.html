jaiélaure.com

jaiél aure is a multidisciplinary artist based in new york city. born on december 1, 2005, in winter park, florida, he was raised between orlando and the bronx. his music blends pop, r&b, and alternative influences, often exploring themes of emotion, memory, and identity.

his work reflects his experiences and perspectives, offering a raw and honest portrayal of his artistic journey. introducing himself to music through garageband at age 7, jaiél began producing in 2012. he released his debut single, “do you?” in 2020, and has since developed a distinctive sound characterized by emotional depth and sonic experimentation, leading to the release of his debut project "Everything and Nothing" which was released in 2022.

his second full-length project, "duae", was released on july 18, 2025. the album marks a shift toward vulnerability, centering on the emotional aftermath of a close, unrequited connection. it blends contemporary pop and r&b elements, showcasing his growth as a producer and storyteller. beyond music, jaiél has expressed interest in fashion, photography, and travel. his work continues to evolve, reflecting his ongoing exploration of self and art.
